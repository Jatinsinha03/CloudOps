CLOUDOPS

SESSION 1

Cloud Ops, short for Cloud Operations, involves managing and optimizing cloud infrastructure, services, and applications. It includes tasks like deployment, monitoring, scaling, and ensuring the reliability and efficiency of cloud-based systems. Cloud Ops teams work to streamline processes, enhance performance, and maintain high availability in cloud environments, contributing to the overall success of cloud-based solutions.
